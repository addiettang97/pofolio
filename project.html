Overview

Led the end-to-end design of a modern e-commerce platform, focusing on streamlining the shopping experience from discovery to checkout. The project involved reimagining product browsing, building a flexible design system, and improving conversion rates through data-informed iterations.

Due to the confidential nature of this work, specific deliverables cannot be shared publicly. Please reach out to learn more about this project in detail.

Research & Discovery

Conducted extensive user research including interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis to identify pain points in the current shopping flow. Synthesized findings into actionable insights that guided the design direction.

Research Findings

Design System

Built a comprehensive design system with reusable components, typography scales, and color tokens. The system ensured consistency across 50+ screens while enabling rapid iteration and handoff to the engineering team.

Design System

Product Pages

Redesigned the product detail pages with a focus on visual hierarchy, trust signals, and clear calls-to-action. Introduced interactive size guides, 360° product views, and contextual recommendations to enhance the shopping experience.

Product Pages

Results & Impact

The redesigned platform launched to positive reception, achieving a 23% increase in conversion rate and a 15% reduction in cart abandonment. The design system continues to scale across new product verticals.
